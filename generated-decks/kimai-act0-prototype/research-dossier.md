# Research Dossier

## Executive Summary

Act 0 introduces the redesigned lecture as a 4-hour general-audience, practice-heavy workshop. The central metaphor is Kimai, a new AI employee who is fast and capable but does not automatically know company context, completion criteria, or verification standards.

## Core Concepts

- Kimai as an AI new hire.
- AI work environment instead of prompt-only instruction.
- Six-step journey: information, prompt, context, skill, agent/tool, hook/evaluation.
- Slides must function as presenter navigation.
